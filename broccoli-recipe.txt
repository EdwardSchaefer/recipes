Ingredients:
-1 small bunch broccoli (about 3/4 pound)
-1 garlic clove
-1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
-1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice

Directions:
1. Discard tough lower third of broccoli stem.
2. Peel remaining stem and cut crosswise into 1/2-inch-thick slices.
3. Cut broccoli into 2-inch florets.
4. In a steamer set over boiling water steam broccoli, covered, until crisp-tender, 4 to 5 minutes.
5. While broccoli is steaming, finely chop garlic and in a small skillet combine with oil, lemon juice, and salt and pepper to taste.
6. Heat garlic mixture over moderate heat until garlic is fragrant.
7. In a bowl toss broccoli with garlic mixture.
